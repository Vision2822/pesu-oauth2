About & Disclaimer

Important Notice

This is NOT an official PES University service. This is an independent student project for educational purposes.

What is PESU OAuth2?

A student-built OAuth2 authentication service that allows PESU students to sign into third-party applications using their PESU Academy credentials—without creating separate accounts for every app.

Think of it like "Sign in with Google" but for PESU students.

Why was this built?

  • To learn OAuth 2.0 authentication flows
  • To practice secure web development
  • To help fellow student developers build PESU-focused apps
  • To contribute to the PESU tech community

This is an educational project, not a commercial service.

Is my password safe?

Yes! We NEVER store your PESU Academy password.

When you login, your credentials go directly to PESU Academy's servers (not ours). PESU Academy validates your password and sends back your profile data. We only store your PRN and profile information—never your password.

What data do you collect?

We collect only what you explicitly consent to share:

  • PESU PRN (your unique identifier)
  • Profile data you authorize (name, email, branch, etc.)
  • OAuth tokens (encrypted, temporary)
  • Usage logs (for security, kept 30 days)
✨ Granular Consent

You choose exactly what data to share with each app. Want to share your email but not your phone number? No problem. You're in complete control.

Your Rights:

  • View all data we have about you
  • Revoke access to any app at any time
  • Request deletion of your data

What about third-party apps?

⚠️ We don't verify third-party applications.
Before authorizing an app, review what data they're requesting. Only grant access to apps you trust. You're responsible for how third-party apps use your data.
